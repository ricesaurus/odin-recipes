Japanese Curry

Description

A simple quick big pot meal to make for when my teeth hurt from braces.

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 1 or 2 carrots
  • 4 cups beef/chicken stock
  • 1 block Java roux
  • 1 block Kokumaro roux
  • Avocado oil
  • Optional: milk and meat
  • Rice to serve

Steps

  1. Dice onions and carrots
  2. Oil the pot and caramelize the onions
  3. Stir fry carrots and meat
  4. Add in broth and wait to boil
  5. Add in curry roux and simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Add in milk and water to taste
