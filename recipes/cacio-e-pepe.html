Cacio E Pepe

Description

Delicious simple italian pasta made with cheese and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 220 g Pecorino Romano
  • 350 g spaghetti
  • 14 g whole black peppercorns
  • 7.5 cups of water
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Grate cheese
  2. Grind peppercorns and toast for 2-3 minutes
  3. Boil water with salt then cook spaghetti for 5 minutes
  4. Add two ladles of pasta water to the peppercorn pan
  5. Finish cooking spaghetti in the pan
  6. Add pasta water to the cheese to make paste
  7. Mix paste in the pan and emulsify and finish cooking pasta
